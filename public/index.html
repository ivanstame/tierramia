Sourcing Spotlight

Finca La Lagunilla

La Lagunilla is actually a group of 45 farmers who banded together in 2003 when the coffee prices were at a significant low on the market. While others abandoned coffee for more profitable crops, the members of La Lagunilla, led by Andres Martinez Leon, decided to go for specialty coffee prices and pushed hard on producing the highest quality.
